About DJ European BOB

DJ European BOB

A multi-genre DJ with a deep-rooted love for the tougher side of dance music

DJ European BOB, delivers high-energy, emotionally charged sets that span

  • trance
  • hard trance
  • techno
  • oldskool, breakbeat, classic house, and warmup grooves.

With a career that began in 1997 and flourished through the golden years of the South Coast scene European BOB made his mark playing across Dorset and running well-received local nights that earned a loyal following..

After stepping back in 2010 to focus on family life, the fire never went out

and now European BOB is back behind the decks with even more passion and perspective.
