🎧 Wildstyle Radio Rules & Regulations

1. General Conduct

All DJs, presenters, and team members must behave respectfully and professionally at all times, turn up for shows on time is a "MUST". Discrimination, harassment, hate speech, or offensive behavior of any kind will not be tolerated. Wildstyle Radio promotes diversity, inclusion, and positive vibes—please reflect this in all interactions.

2. Broadcast Guidelines

Shows must start and end on time unless prior arrangements have been made. Explicit content (including language, themes, or music) must be clearly marked and must comply with platform broadcasting regulations. DJs must not promote illegal activity or misinformation on air.

3. Music & Content

Wildstyle Radio holds a broadcasting license that allows us to play both signed (commercial) and unsigned (independent) music. DJs are encouraged to maintain a balanced and diverse playlist, showcasing both popular tracks and emerging talent.

While our license covers most music, DJs must avoid uploading or distributing music outside of the station without proper rights. Plagiarism or passing off someone else’s work as your own is strictly prohibited.

4. Technical Responsibilities

DJs must test their equipment before each show and report any issues promptly. Do not make unauthorized changes to station settings, stream links, or shared resources. Backup music and emergency playlists must be available in case of connection failure.

5. Promotion & Branding

Use the official Wildstyle Radio branding when promoting your show. Personal show pages or promotions must link back to the Wildstyle Radio website or social media. Any merchandise or third-party promotions must be approved by management.

6. Scheduling & Communication

DJs must stick to their scheduled times and notify management of absences with at least 24 hours’ notice. Use the official communication channels for updates, scheduling, and emergencies. Failure to communicate repeated absences may result in removal from the schedule.

7. Listener Interaction

Be polite and professional when interacting with listeners through chat, social media, or live call-ins. Do not reveal personal or confidential information on air. Encourage positive and respectful engagement in all community spaces.

8. Management Rights

Management reserves the right to review all shows and content. Violations of these rules may lead to a warning, suspension, or removal from the station. All decisions by Wildstyle Radio management are final.

9. Important Note

Wildstyle Radio does not accept drunks onto this station. This is a professional platform committed to quality broadcasting and respectful collaboration.
