Station Rules & Guidelines

🎧 Wildstyle Radio Rules & Regulations

1. General Conduct

All DJs, presenters, and team members must behave respectfully and professionally at all times. Turning up on time is a MUST. No discrimination, hate speech, or toxic behaviour.

2. Broadcast Guidelines

Shows must start and end on time. Explicit content must be clearly marked. No illegal activity or misinformation.

3. Music & Content

We are licensed to play signed and unsigned music. Support new artists, keep variety, and don’t distribute music illegally.

4. Technical Responsibilities

Test your setup before going live. No messing with stream settings. Always have backup playlists ready.

5. Promotion & Branding

Use official branding. Link back to Wildstyle. Get approval before promoting external stuff.

6. Scheduling & Communication

Stick to your slot. Give 24 hours notice if you’re off. No ghosting.

7. Listener Interaction

Respect listeners. Keep it friendly. No personal info sharing.

8. Management Rights

We can review shows and remove DJs if rules are broken.

9. Important Note

No drunk DJs on air. Keep it professional.
