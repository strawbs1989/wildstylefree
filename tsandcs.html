Station Rules & Guidelines

Standard procedures for all DJs, presenters, and crew members.

Important Zero-Tolerance Note

No drunk DJs on air. Keep it clean, keep it crisp, and keep it professional.

01. General Conduct

All DJs, presenters, and team members must behave respectfully and professionally at all times. Turning up on time is a MUST. Absolutely zero discrimination, hate speech, or toxic behavior will be tolerated.

02. Broadcast Guidelines

Shows must start and end precisely on time to ensure smooth handovers. Explicit content must be clearly marked. No promotion of illegal activities or misinformation.

03. Music & Content

We are fully licensed to play signed and unsigned tracks. Actively support upcoming artists, keep your music selection varied, and never distribute or download copyrighted tracks illegally.

04. Technical Duties

Test your audio levels, bitrates, and hardware setup before going live. Do not alter core stream settings. Always keep a backup local playlist ready in case connection drops.

05. Promotion & Branding

Always utilize current official Wildstyle assets. Drop regular station liners and link back to the hub. Management approval is strictly required before promoting external brands or merchandise.

06. Schedule & Cover

Commit strictly to your assigned time slots. Provide at least 24 hours notice in the crew channels if you need show coverage. No sudden ghosting or unannounced absences.

07. Crew & Fan Interaction

Treat listeners with patience and respect across all chat interactions. Keep room banter light and engaging. Never share confidential personal information or internal management issues publicly.

08. Management Rights

Station management reserves the full right to monitor broadcasts, audit logs, and remove contributors from rotation if any of these regulations are continuously ignored.
