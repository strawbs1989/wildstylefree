Services

Do you want to volunteer with us as a DJ? We can help you get everything set up so you can broadcast live on Wildstyle Radio.

We offer help and support with the following broadcasting software:

🎙 B.U.T.T Broadcaster

Perfect if you're new to internet radio. B.U.T.T is free, lightweight and very easy to configure. We'll help you connect it to the Wildstyle Radio stream.

🎧 SAM Broadcaster

A professional broadcasting package with playlists, cart players and advanced scheduling. We'll help you configure it and connect it to Wildstyle Radio.

🎵 VirtualDJ

Ideal for live DJ mixes and club-style shows. We'll help you configure the audio settings and broadcast your mixes live on Wildstyle Radio.
