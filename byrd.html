About The Byrdman

Eddie Byrd, aka “The Byrdman,” began his broadcast career in 1970 in Aiken,

South Carolina, USA. Over the years, he has hosted.

and worked in both radio and television broadcasting,

Top 40, Country, Classic Rock, And, Adult Contemporary formats.

though radio has always been his true passion.
