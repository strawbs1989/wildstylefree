Services

Do you want to volunteer with us as a DJ? We can help you get everything set up so you can broadcast live on Wildstyle Radio.

We offer help and support with the following broadcasting software:

🎙 B.U.T.T Broadcaster

Perfect if you're new to internet radio. B.U.T.T is free, lightweight and very easy to configure. We'll help you connect it to the Wildstyle Radio stream.

🎧 SAM Broadcaster

A professional broadcasting package with playlists, cart players and advanced scheduling. We'll help you configure it and connect it to Wildstyle Radio.

🎵 VirtualDJ

Ideal for live DJ mixes and club-style shows. We'll help you configure the audio settings and broadcast your mixes live on Wildstyle Radio.

🤝 We'll Help You Every Step of the Way

Don't worry if you've never broadcast before. Our experienced team can help you get everything ready. From installing software to your very first live show, we'll be there to support you.

  • ✅ Installing broadcasting software
  • ✅ Connecting to the Wildstyle stream
  • ✅ Microphone and audio setup
  • ✅ Music quality advice
  • ✅ Going live for the first time
  • ✅ Ongoing technical support

Q&As

Is this voluntary?

Yes. Wildstyle Radio is run entirely by volunteers who simply love music and broadcasting.

Is There Payment Involved?

No! This Is Completely Free And Voluntary!

If I Recommend Friends Are They Allowed To Join?

Yes. We Accept Anyone onboard!

Can anyone join?

Yes. We welcome anyone who is very passionate about music, or want to get there music out there.

If I join Can We Stream into Virtual Worlds.

Yes You Can Stream With Wildstyle in Virtual Worlds.

I'm new to broadcasting. Will someone help me?

Absolutely! Our technical team can help you install software, configure your stream and get you ready for your first live show.

When is explicit or adult music allowed?

Tracks containing strong language or adult content should only be played after the watershed at 9:00 PM (UK time).
